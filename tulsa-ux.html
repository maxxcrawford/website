Tulsa UX

I founded Tulsa UX in July of 2017. This group – the first UX-focused meetup in Tulsa – is sponsored by Techlahoma. At Tulsa UX, local Tulsans meet up monthly to learn more about best UX practices, the latest design trends and how best to continue educating themselves on the subject.
